About Me!

Hey, I’m Michael! I’m 21 and a mix of Malaysian-Chinese and Hungarian. I moved to Canada when I was 5 and grew up between Calgary and Vancouver.

I have ADHD, which means my brain is always bouncing between new ideas, hyperfixations, and random deep dives into things that interest me. When I’m not gaming into the late hours or working on my car, I’m probably obsessing over cats, finding new music, or getting lost in internet rabbit holes.

I love creating, and figuring things out—sometimes all at once. Let’s connect!

Let’s get technical

I’m an Interactive Designer with a focus on UI/UX, user research, and digital experiences. My work revolves around creating intuitive, functional, and engaging designs that prioritize user needs. With a background in interactive design, branding, and content strategy, I bring a mix of creativity and problem-solving to every project.

I have experience in wireframing, prototyping, and front-end development, working with tools like Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, WordPress, and HTML/CSS. Beyond design, I also have a passion for photography, videography, and digital storytelling, often capturing my love for cars through visual content.
